Our Story

Afriharvest Beef is a Tanzanian Beef Production Company nestled in the vast savannah plains of central Tanzania. With the fascinating Miombo woodlands and the lush green grass of the savannah, our farm is perfectly suited to raise indigenous Tanzanian cattle; the Shorthorn Zebu that thrives in the local climate. Our carefully designed and formulated feeds which are organically grown in our farms, provides optimal nutrition and promote healthy growth, resulting in beef of top notch quality. Beyond beef cattle, we also rear goats and sheep which are very much adapted in feeding from the shrubs. This diversification allows us to offer a wide range of high-quality meat products to meet the diverse needs of our customers. We also provide consultancy services in beef production with our objective to assist interested investors venture into beef production. At our farm, we share our experiences, expertise, and knowledge on how to establish and manage successful feedlots.
