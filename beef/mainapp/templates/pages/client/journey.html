Our Journey

Our commitment to quality ensures the availability of an elevated premium beef. This is the story of how we make our beef from the farm to your fork.

The Tanzanian Way

Our commitment to raising indigenous cattle, coupled with our high standards of production, ensure our cattle are healthy, well catered for and shows the world that local Tanzanian breeds can produce high quality meat products.

Feed and Nutrition

We feed our cattle with our own home-grown fodder which includes the grown Rhodes grass, Napier grass and Lucerne. We grow our own grains which we use in formulating our feeds with supplementation from our neighboring local community of farmers to ensure they are fed a balanced and nutrient rich diet that meets all their needs.

Automated Processes for Consistency & Efficiency

We take pride in using automated processes to ensure consistency and efficiency in all our operations. From feeding to processing our feeds, our systems are designed to provide the best possible results.

Professionalism at Heart

We believe that being part of the food industry is both a privilege and a responsibility, this is why we try to be the best in all areas of production we embark in. We have both skillful trained and unskilled personnel whom we adequately train to ensure that we maintain exceptional standards of hygiene through all stages of production ensuring our clients receive fresh premium beef full of natural flavors.

Come Visit Us & Experience Quality Beef Raised with Care

Afriharvest Beef has invested in acquiring and utilizing knowledge, mechanization, and sustainable farming to ensure that final products that lands on your plate is fresh, tender, marble, juicy and bursting with natural flavors. We intend to redefine beef farming, and now, beef is Afriharvest Beef.
